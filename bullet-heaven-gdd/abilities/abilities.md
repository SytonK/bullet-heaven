the player will have a starting ability that can damage enemies and mine some stones.
the player will also learn new abilities throughout the game and will upgrade them.
after an ability have been upgraded enough times there will be a special upgrade, the player will choose one of a few options
a few times in a run the player may choose to combine 2 fully upgraded abilities to a new stronger ability

abilities can be also upgraded globally outside of a run
![[ability combination sketch.jpg]]
ability types:
1. fire
2. water
3. dark
4. light

abilities:
starting ability: axes? (starting ability) - damage near player and brake stone

fire (burn damage):
1. meteor - drop meteors from the sky
2. (duration) lava - creates lava zones
3.  (creature) phoenix - summons a phoenix to attack
4. dragon breath - breath fire to enemies in front

water (slows enemies):
1. (creature) water elemental - summons a water elemental to attack
2.  tsunami - create tsunami towards the players front
3. (duration) ice zone - create a zone with erupting icebergs  
4. ice shards - creates ice shards around the player

light (buff player):
1. (duration) energy field - creates energy fields that rotate around the player
2. divine sword - attack enemies in front of the player
3. lightning - create lighting towards the closest enemies
4. (creature) angel - summons an angle to attack

dark (debuff, take extra damage):
1. dark shard - throw a dark shard towards the closest enemy 
2. (creature) demon - summons a demon to attack enemies
3. dark sword - attack enemies in front of the player
4. (duration) doom - summons a dark field that moves and damages enemies

combinations:
fire & fire:
1. meteor + lava - meteor that leaves lava
2. dragon breath + phoenix - dragon

water & water:
1. water element + tsunami - water dragon that spits waves
2. ice zone + ice shard - ice zone with ice shards on every iceberg explosion

light + light:
1. energy field + divine sword - summon swords that rotate around the player
2. lightning + angel - an angel that attack summons lightnings that splits to other enemies

dark & dark:
3. dark shard + demon - a demon that throws giant shard at enemies
4. dark sword + doom - a sword that creates a dooms wave every 3rd attack

fire & water:
1. meteor + ice shard - a small meteor shards that target the closest enemy
2. lava + ice zone - earthquake zone with lava and icebergs
3. phoenix + tsunami - phoenix that summons tsunami of lava
4. dragon breath + water elemental - crystal elemental 

fire & light:
1. meteor + angel - a meteor that summons spirits to fight for you
2. lava + lightning - a lava zone with lightnings
3. phoenix + divine sword - summon an angel that attacks with flaming swords that create waves of fire
4. dragon breath + energy field - breath fire  that rotates around the player

fire & dark:
1. meteor + doom - a huge meteor that leaves many small dark fields
2. lava + dark sword - a sword that creates lava waves every 4th attack
3. phoenix + demon - summon a tiny flaming demon
4. dragon breath + dark shard - dark breath

water & light:
1. water element + angel - water dragon
2. tsunami + lightning - storm
3. ice zone + divine sword - ice sword that creates an iceberg every 3rd attack. breaking the iceberg explodes it, creating an ice zone
4. ice shard + energy field - an ice shard that rotate around the player as he moves
