top down 2d game where in a run the player fight waves of enemies and get abilities that auto activate. the player needs to mine stones from the ground to not only make a path but also to get stronger
after every run the player gain out of game resources that unlock  and upgrade abilities and stats

genres:
1. bullet-heaven
2. rogue-light

graphics:
very simple graphics, mainly shapes and icons / sketches / dark demonic
